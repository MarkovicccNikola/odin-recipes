Chicken and Rice in The Oven

Description

This is the standard, go-to bodybuilder meal. It has plenty of protein and carbs, and if you make it this way it will also taste amazing

Ingrediets

  • 200g chicken breast
  • 100g brown rice
  • 1 medium onion
  • Some seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C
  2. Put in the chicken breast and give it 30 minutes
  3. Prepare the rice, I don't know how that is done
  4. Chop the onion and throw it over the chicken breast
  5. Put everything on the plate and season it
